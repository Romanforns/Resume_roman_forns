Migration and refactoring of the TOUS data platform (GPC_Tous). REST APIs (recommendations, abandoned cart, wishlist, views, Hybris), data pipelines (SFTP landing via Dataflow, Salesforce, Google Analytics, Facebook, Hybris, loyalty), Customer 360 view, exports to Datastore and Marketing Cloud. Recommendation system (73+ use cases, SVD model). Deployed on Google Cloud (BigQuery, Datastore, Dataflow, Pub/Sub, Composer/Airflow).
